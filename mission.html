Our Mission is to Protect Environment

SPVAIG Automobiles Private Limited was set up in 2023 by entrepreneurs who have a passion for the automotive world and technology and they are convinced that the future of mobility is going to be 100% electric. The days of combustion engines used in vehicles are numbered. Our current world is immersed in an exciting process of change, which will culminate in the mass adoption of electric vehicles. This is how we will eventually be able to travel around in a sustainable and environmentally friendly way. SPVAIG Automobiles Private Limited not only wants to be part of this transition, but we also want to lead it by creating new technology, materials, and business models to be able to provide our customers with the best electric scooters on the market.



Our Vision

The Government wants India to be a 100% electric vehicle nation by the year 2030. Under the new plan of the government, every car sold in India from 2030 will be electric. Due to the competitive market, India’s auto industry has become one of the largest in the world. As per the Review Report of Automotive Mission Plan 2016, the turnover of the auto industry is equivalent to 7.1%. The government of India approved the National Mission on Electric Mobility in 2011 and later the National Electric Mobility Mission Plan (NEMMP) 2020 in 2013.
