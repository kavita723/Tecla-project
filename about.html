SPVAIG Automobiles Private Limited, established in 2023, is a leading manufacturer of electric scooters and bikes in India. We also have an extensive network of dealers and sub-dealers.

Currently, our operations are focused in India, but we have plans for global expansion in the near future. We take pride in owning our own chassis, painting, and assembly factories, which allows us to maintain strict quality control. With our independent research and development capabilities, as well as a comprehensive range of testing equipment, we ensure that our products meet the highest standards of quality.

Our product range includes two- and three-wheel electric scooters, motorcycles, bikes, and three-wheel cargo motorcycles. In the coming years, we also have plans to venture into the manufacturing of four-wheelers.

At SPVAIG Automobiles, we are committed to providing the best options for eco-friendly transportation. Our electric two-wheelers are designed to offer a seamless and pollution-free riding experience. We have incorporated cutting-edge technology into our high-speed electric scooters, ensuring that you enjoy a smooth and efficient ride.



SPVAIG Automobiles Private Limited was set up in 2023 by entrepreneurs who have a passion for the automotive world and technology and they are convinced that the future of mobility is going to be 100% electric.
